Introduction to the new Figment
 

Welcome to the brand new Figment!


Figment.com recently became "Underlined", and screwed over tons of writers who made a lot of friends on it.
My brother, Moses Lingle, truly appreciated Figment and was sad to see it go.
I, (FHGDev), being a web developer, decided to remake Figment, and provide a place for writers 14+ to collaborate on their stories, poems, and more!



Figment was recoded and redesigned by FHGDev.
